    Located in Palo Alto, California, Tencent America is dedicated to bringing high quality and engaging gaming and mobile experiences to our users. Come meet the people who make Tencent America a great place to work!

  • FAQ

    Q: Where is Tencent America located?
    A: Our office is located in downtown Palo Alto, California at 661 Bryant Street.

    Q: Are there any other Tencent offices in the U.S.?
    A: Yes, there is also a Tencent office in Boston. Check out their website (www.tencentboston.com) for additional information.

    Q: I cannot speak Chinese. Do I need to speak Chinese to apply for jobs at Tencent America?
    A: Generally no. Most of our jobs do not require Chinese language skills.

    Q: I am an international student. Does Tencent America sponsor H1b visa?
    A: In certain circumstances, we do sponsor H1b visas. The decision is based on many factors including job needs, special skills of applicants and budget. Our recruiters can provide more information regarding visas and specific positions.

    Q: I am interested in Tencent job opportunities in China. How can I apply?
    A: Please visit hr.tencent.com and apply online.
